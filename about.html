Company Introduction

Kapse Engineering Works

Kapse Engineering provides mechanical engineering services, CNC machining services, tool room work, dynamic balancing service, industrial automation services and heavy machine maintenance from Waluj MIDC, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar.

Our focus is simple: understand the machine, understand the production requirement, and deliver reliable workmanship that helps customers keep operations moving.

  • Precision manufacturing and custom machine parts
  • Industrial maintenance, field balancing and vibration analysis
  • Tool room capability for dies, holders, fixtures and repair work
  • Quality-driven processes for repeatable results
